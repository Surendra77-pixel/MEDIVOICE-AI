# 🎥 MediVoice AI — Demo & Feature Walkthrough

> **A visual guide to the platform's core capabilities.**

---

## 🏥 1. Patient Portal
- **Dashboard**: Real-time health stats and upcoming appointments.
- **AI Symptom Chatbot**: Powered by HuggingFace. Detects symptoms and suggests the correct specialist.
- **SOS Button**: Persistent emergency trigger for critical cases.
- **Doctor Search**: Geo-location based search for nearby specialists.

## 🩺 2. Doctor Portal
- **Consultation Room**: 
  - Real-time **Speech-to-Text** (Multilingual).
  - AI-driven **SOAP Notes** generation.
  - Professional **PDF Prescription** issuing.
- **Queue Management**: Intelligent risk-based sorting of waiting patients.
- **Analytics**: Clinical performance and patient risk distribution charts.

## 🛡️ 3. Admin Portal
- **Control Center**: System health metrics and security audit logs.
- **User Governance**: Account status controls and doctor credential verification.
- **Security Monitor**: Real-time tracking of failed login attempts and potential threats.

---

## 🚀 Advanced Tech Highlights
- **100% Free Infrastructure**: Optimized to run on MongoDB Atlas, Render, and Vercel free tiers.
- **PWA support**: Full mobile installation support with service workers.
- **Security**: JWT-based authentication with high-entropy entropy and RBAC.
